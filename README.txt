HOW TO USE:

1. Open index.html in your browser
2. Click "Get Started" or "Sign Up"
3. Create an account with your name, email, and password
4. You'll be redirected to the dashboard
5. Click "Create Survey" to build your first survey
6. Add questions using the sidebar (text, rating, multiple choice, etc.)
7. Save and preview your survey
8. Generate QR code to share with customers
9. View responses and analytics in the dashboard
